In April 2018, Triple Edge Studios released the Diamond Empire three-reel slot machine. 15 lines in its structure turned out to be enough for frequent prize combinations. The presence of bonus options guarantee 96% return on loans spent.

Gaming machine review Diamond Empire

In the Diamond Empire video slot implemented the theme of jewelry. Diamonds sparkle not only on the drums, but also around them. The main background is a carved board. It depicts charming girls. In an intricate ornament on the edges of the screen you can see gems of different sizes. The symbols of the video slot are diamonds, cherries, number 7, the inscription BAR in several variations, the wheel of fortune with multi-colored sectors. Prize combinations are formed only from 3 identical pictograms. Other options do not apply.

To control the Diamond Empire online machine, no special skills are required. The button on the center is used to activate the reels. She also stops the game ahead of schedule. To change the number of credits, the Bet key is provided, as well as the Max Bet, in order to play at the maximum bet. In the video slot, all lines are active, so their number does not change. To start automatically, use the Auto Play button. At the left edge of the screen there is a menu with advanced settings buttons.

Symbols in the slot machine

Extend the scope of the gameplay in the Diamond Empire online slot machine symbols with unique features. Brilliant used as a wild sign. He is able to independently build prize combinations that bring the greatest profit. It also helps to complete winning combinations by replacing missing icons. At the same time, one wild doubles payouts, and 2 doubles the amount by 4 times. There is a scatter in the slot machine. His appearance provides a chance to participate in the prize tour.

The Diamond Empire bonus game is activated with the advent of 3 Bonus signs. It is the wheel of fortune. The round involves the start of the wheel, where the sectors are multiplication factors. The opportunity to do 1 rotation. The total bet is multiplied by the won score.

Game slot Diamond Empire will appeal to all lovers of the classics. But the presence of the bonus round and the wild sign with the multiplication option characterize it as a modern model. Prize features guarantee solid cash payments.
