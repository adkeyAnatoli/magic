Slot machine Lucky Firecracker

Categories: Online Slot Reviews | Published by: anetka
29/01/2024

Are you ready to celebrate the Chinese New Year, blow up crackers, launch lanterns and fireworks into the sky? If the soul requires a holiday, turn on the new Lucky Firecracker gaming machine from Microgaming, magical themed graphics, Chinese-style music and animation when winning compositions appear on the reels.

Betting range

Before starting the reels, each player chooses the desired bet size. On this slot machine you can use from one to ten coins with a denomination of 0.01-1 credit. Here users with different bankroll will be able to play, since the maximum bet in the game is 250 credits, and the minimum is 0.25.

For the most lazy players, the “Auto Play” mode is also provided, in which you can set the required number of automated spins and play comfortably and relaxedly.

Symbols and interface of the game

The Lucky Firecracker slot machine consists of five reels and offers lovers of gambling 243 potentially winning combinations. The game has a standard interface with an intuitive english control panel. All the basic information about the rules and payout ratios for a winning composition can be found in a special information table.

Among the symbols of this video slot there are images of clappers, fireworks, lanterns and sparklers, as well as multi-colored senior playing cards.

Features and bonus games

Among the special symbols of the machine, it is impossible not to mention the scatter, depicted as a Chinese coin and a wild symbol – an icon with hieroglyphs.

The scatter symbol is paid in any position on the reels, regardless of the active gaming lines. If three or more icons fall out, users can access the bonus round with 15 bonus spins.

The wild symbol appears only on the 2nd and 4th reels and has the function of replacing other icons (with the exception of the scatter), forming winning bundles on the reels.

Lucky Firecracker offers the most gambling video slot players to take part in the risk game. Here you can double the amount of your winnings by correctly guessing the color of the closed playing card. Let fortune be on your side!
